PAC Music — Global Music Education Platform

PAC Music is a platform concept designed to connect music students and educators globally through accessible, flexible, and meaningful learning experiences.

Project Overview

PAC Music was developed as a business and platform concept focused on music education. The idea was to create a space where students could find the right teachers more easily, while teachers could also benefit from a more structured and supportive platform.

Instead of thinking about music education only as one-on-one lessons, this project considered the bigger picture: platform experience, business model, user needs, and how to make music learning feel more connected and accessible.

My Role

In this project, I took a leading role in shaping the overall concept and direction. I focused on the business side of the platform and helped define how the idea could work in a realistic and scalable way.

  • Developed the core platform concept
  • Explored pricing and revenue model ideas
  • Thought through the structure for both students and teachers
  • Analyzed how the platform could create value for different users
  • Helped shape the brand and overall positioning of the idea

What This Project Shows

PAC Music reflects my interest in work that combines creativity with structure. It shows how I think about ideas not only from an artistic perspective, but also from a user, business, and platform point of view.

More specifically, this project demonstrates my interest in:

  • Creative business development
  • Platform and product thinking
  • Music education and community building
  • Marketing and brand direction
  • Scalable ideas within the music industry

Why It Matters to Me

This project is meaningful to me because it connects several things I care about: music, education, accessibility, and long-term creative impact. It also reflects the kind of work I hope to keep doing in the future — building ideas that are thoughtful, useful, and rooted in real human needs.
